Our Business Model

Agric Solutions Ltd operates with a unique and sustainable business model designed to benefit both investors and the local community. From our humble beginnings in the mountains of Leribe, Hleoheng, we combined modern livestock farming with community-centered initiatives to create a profitable and socially responsible agricultural company.

How We Operate

  1. Animal Feed Production: We produce high-quality, organic feed for cows, pigs, goats, and sheep, ensuring healthier livestock and higher yields.
  2. Livestock Services: Our team provides veterinary care, artificial insemination, and crossbreeding services to improve herd quality.
  3. Meat Supply: Premium meat is supplied to local restaurants and butcheries, categorized by grade for diverse customer needs.
  4. Wool & Mohair Collection: We harvest wool and mohair from goats and sheep and sell to textile markets and craft industries.
  5. Community Soil Fertility Programs: Organic waste from our animals is recycled as fertilizer to improve local farmland, strengthening our connection with surrounding communities.

Revenue Streams

  • Sales of animal feed to farmers and livestock owners
  • Provision of veterinary and livestock services
  • Sale of meat products to restaurants and butcheries
  • Sale of wool and mohair to textile and craft markets
  • Investment returns from public shareholders who own a stake in the company

What Makes Us Unique

Unlike traditional farms, Agric Solutions Ltd combines profitable operations with environmental and community responsibility. By improving soil fertility in nearby lands, promoting sustainable livestock practices, and opening shareholding to the public, we ensure that our growth benefits both investors and the local community. Our strong focus on organic production, animal welfare, and innovation positions us as a leader in modern agricultural solutions in Lesotho.
