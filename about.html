ABOUT AGRI SOLUTION LTD

Agric Solutions Ltd was founded by Neo Masilo, a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to transform livestock farming in Lesotho. The idea started as a simple solution to improve animal health and productivity, and over time it grew into a full-fledged agricultural company. Neo Masilo successfully pitched the concept to investors and opened shares to the public, allowing the community to participate in the growth of the business and helping raise startup capital.

Our company is strategically based in the mountains of Leribe, Hleoheng. This location provides us with abundant water resources, ample arable land, and isolation from nearby villages to prevent complaints about livestock odors. The surrounding land also allows us to contribute to the community by spreading organic fertilizer from our animal waste, improving soil fertility and supporting local agriculture.

OUR MISSION

At Agric Solutions Ltd, our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainable agricultural solutions that empower farmers and communities. We aim to improve livestock health and productivity through innovative feeding programs, veterinary care, and artificial insemination services. Additionally, we are committed to environmental sustainability by recycling organic waste to enrich local soils, contributing to food security and a greener future. Our mission extends beyond business profit — we strive to create a positive social impact and strengthen the livelihoods of the communities we serve.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become the leading agricultural solutions provider in Lesotho and the broader Southern African region. We envision a future where every farmer has access to modern, sustainable livestock solutions, improving productivity and profitability. Agric Solutions Ltd aspires to set the standard for ethical, environmentally-conscious farming practices while building a strong, inclusive community of investors, farmers, and partners. Ultimately, we aim to innovate continuously, expanding our services to meet the growing demands of modern agriculture and ensuring that our solutions leave a lasting impact on both people and the planet.
